EDGARS

Edgars is a Johannesburg-based chain of stores present all over Southern Africa. The department store specialises in selling clothing, shoes, homeware & beauty and under great leadership The chain had just around 200 stores, with multiple locations in South Africa, Namibia, Zambia and Botswana as well as in the capital cities of eSwatini (Swaziland), Lesotho, and Ghana when it was sold to Durban based, private fashion company Retailability in 2020.
